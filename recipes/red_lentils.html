Red lentil soup

Description

This is a recipe for red lentil soup taken from Marcela Hazan's Classical Italian Cooking. It is not a family recipe (obviously), but it is such a great recipe I had to share it.

Ingredients

  • red lentils
  • minced onion
  • roma tomatos
  • finely chopped celery
  • finely chopped carrot
  • butter and oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. throw in the mix of butter and oil (1:1 ratio)
  2. add the onion and let cook until about golden brown
  3. add the celery and carrot
  4. add the tomato
  5. add vegetable stock, salt, and pepper
