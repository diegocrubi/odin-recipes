Copo

Description

The copo, or granisado, is a tasty Costa Rican dessert, consisting largely of shaved ice and condensed milk!

Ingredients

  • Shaved Ice
  • Grenadine syrup
  • condensed milk
  • powdered milk
  • transparent cup

Steps

  1. Add some shaved ice to about 1/3rd of the transparent cup
  2. Add some grenadine syrup to the base layer of shaved ice
  3. Add some powdered milk to form the next layer
  4. Add some condensed milk to form the next layer
  5. Repeat until you get to the top of the cup!
